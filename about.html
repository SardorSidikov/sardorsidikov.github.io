What is a WBS project

WBS of a project (Work Breakdown Structure) is a breakdown of a project into specific results that must be achieved to achieve the goals of the project.

"a rule, the project itself is indicated at the top level, below it (at the first level) are the main results, each of which, in turn, is detailed, that is, the next level is always smaller than the previous one in terms of the volume of work and, as a rule, includes two or more work packages. Moreover, in different branches of WBS there may be a different number of levels depending on the desired degree of detail."

Developer S.Sidikov
